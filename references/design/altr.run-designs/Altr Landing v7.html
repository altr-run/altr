Magic-link onboarding

owner: @mukul · co-authored with @spec

The problem

Teams sign up, hit the password step, and 34% bounce before their first workspace. We lose them before we learn anything. We want an invite flow that feels like opening a door, not filling out a form.

Acceptance criteria

  • Any teammate can be invited by email alone
  • Invites deliver within 10 seconds of send
  • Expired invites (7 days) surface a resend CTA
  • Rate-limit: 5 invites / user / hour
  • Audit log records inviter, invitee, status

§ 04 · straight answers

Questions we hear weekly.

If yours isn't here, write hello@altr.run — a human reads every one.

Is Altr Mac-only?+
For now, yes. Mac-native, not Electron. Windows and Linux are on the 2027 roadmap. We'd rather ship one great client than three mediocre ones.
Where do my files live?+
On your Mac, encrypted at rest. Agents call models over your API keys; nothing becomes training data. Full details in the security whitepaper.
Which models power the teammates?+
@spec runs on Claude Sonnet. @eng wraps Claude Code. BYO keys, or use ours with $20 of credits per seat per month on Studio.
How is this different from Devin?+
Devin is an autonomous cloud engineer. Altr is a workspace where humans and several agents collaborate on your machine, with humans in the driver's seat. Different problem, different shape.
When is public launch?+
Private beta June 2026, public launch Q4 2026. Request access to join the beta cohort.
Do you have SOC 2?+
Type II audit in progress; report expected Q3 2026. Type I and DPA available today for Studio and Scale customers.
Can I self-host?+
Scale customers can run Altr on a dedicated instance, on-prem, or in a VPC of their choice. Talk to sales for specifics.
