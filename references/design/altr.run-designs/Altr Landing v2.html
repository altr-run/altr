Magic-link auth for first-time users

Owner @mukul  ·  Drafting with Pax  ·  Updated 2m ago

When a new user lands on the signup page, they enter their email and receive a one-time magic link. Clicking the link creates their account and drops them into the product without a password step.

Acceptance criteria — Link expires after 15 minutes. Clicking an expired link surfaces a "send new link" CTA. Rate-limit: 3 requests / email / hour. Resend Webhook logs events to Linear.
Constraints. No third-party auth provider. Use Supabase magic-link primitives. Email subject line must match brand voice (see brand.md).

§ 04 · what we believe

Two bets.

The whole product falls out of two convictions. If either is wrong, Altr is wrong.
Bet 01

A team of three will ship like a team of nine.

The next wave of great software will be built by tiny, AI-native teams where every member — human or agent — is senior, sharp, and on the same page. Altr is the workspace for that team.

Bet 02

Integration cost will kill 90% of agent products.

Agents that live in their own tab, with their own memory, their own UI, their own auth — they multiply friction. Altr puts all the agents inside the tool you're already using, with shared context by default.

§ 07 · questions

Things people ask.

Straight answers. If yours isn't here, email hey@altr.run.
01
Is this a Mac-only app?
For now, yes. Mac-native, not Electron. Windows and Linux are on the roadmap but not in 2026. We'd rather ship one great client than three mediocre ones.
02
Where do my files go?
They stay on your Mac. Specs, code, conversations — all stored locally, encrypted at rest. Agents call out to models over your keys; your files never become training data.
03
Which models power the teammates?
Pax uses Claude. Eng wraps Claude Code. Dex will offer a choice of Claude or GPT image models. You can bring your own keys, or use ours (rate-limited on the free tier).
04
Why a new app instead of a Linear / Cursor plugin?
Because the unit of work is the loop, not the ticket. Plugins leave you tab-switching. Altr puts spec, chat, and code in one window with shared context — the thing a plugin structurally can't do.
05
What's the team behind this?
Three humans and four agents. We're shipping Altr with Altr — Pax wrote half of this page, Eng shipped the auth flow you'll use to sign in.
06
Can I try it before I pay?
Yes. 14-day free trial, no card. Or reserve a Founding Member seat ($499 lifetime) and get first access + a monthly call with us.
07
Will teammates replace my team?
No. They replace your tab-switching. A senior PM is still a senior PM; Pax makes them twice as fast at the boring parts so they can do the hard parts.
08
How is this different from Devin?
Devin is an autonomous engineer in the cloud. Altr is a workspace where a human and several agents collaborate on your machine, with the human in the driver's seat. Different problem.
09
When does it ship?
Private beta June 2026. Public launch Q4 2026. Founding Members get access the day we turn it on.
