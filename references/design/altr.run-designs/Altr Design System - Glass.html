01 — Pillars

Brand pillars

Three statements. The glass version inherits the same soul — the material is different, not the beliefs.

I

Altr is a room, not a robot — a place you and your AI teammates sit down to do real work.

II

Altr is craft at new scale — the care of a small studio, the reach of a full team.

III

Altr is quiet on purpose — confidence without noise, intelligence without theatre.

06 — Logo

Wordmark exploration

The marks themselves do not wear glass — that would betray the brief and the logo. What changes is the icon fill: we have a new Liquid-Glass-style app-icon treatment for the Threshold direction that plays with Apple's new icon system.

C · PICK · GLASS ICON

Threshold — a room, with and without glass

The mark is unchanged. The app-icon shell now offers three finishes: warm frosted (tinted glass), solid ink (classic), and vapor (ambient color). Each is a legitimate Liquid-Glass appearance.
